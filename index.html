What is Augmented Reality?

A technology that overlays digital information such as images, sounds, videos, or 3D objects onto the real world.

Immersive Experience

AR allows users to interact with 3D models in their real-world environment, visualizing cats in their homes or outdoor spaces for better-informed adoption decisions.

Convenience

AR is accessible through mobile devices, enabling users to explore and interact with cats anytime, anywhere, without the need for specialized equipment.

Interactive Decision-Making

AR empowers users to zoom, rotate, and explore the 3D models from various angles, providing a more detailed understanding of the cats before adoption.

Increased Engagement

Incorporating AR sparks curiosity and creates a fun, educational tool to engage users with the adoption process and stray animal management in a creative way.

*Please note that the 3D models may appear different from the preview images, as certain visual elements, such as hair particles, are not supported in the current file format.

How to Use AR

1. Click the "Start AR" button to begin.
2. Allow camera access when prompted.
3. Point your camera at a flat surface to place the 3D cat.
4. Use touch gestures to zoom, rotate, or move the model.
Note: It is recommended to experience AR using your mobile phones.
