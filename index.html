What is Augmented Reality?

A technology that overlays digital information such as images, sounds, videos, or 3D objects onto the real world.

How to Use AR

1. Click the "Start AR" button to begin.
2. Allow camera access when prompted.
3. Point your camera at a flat surface to place the 3D cat.
4. Use touch gestures to zoom, rotate, or move the model.
Note: It is recommended to experience AR using your mobile phones.
